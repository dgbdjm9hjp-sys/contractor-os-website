Cookie Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function and can provide useful analytics information.

2. Cookies we may use

  • Strictly necessary cookies for core website and account functionality.
  • Preference cookies to remember settings.
  • Performance or analytics cookies to improve website and product experience (where used).

3. Consent

Where legally required, non-essential cookies are used only after consent. Essential cookies may be used without consent because they are required for service operation.

4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through browser settings, including deleting existing cookies and blocking future cookies. Some features may not function correctly if essential cookies are blocked.

5. Third-party tools

If we use third-party analytics or communication tools, they may set cookies according to their own policies. We review providers to maintain appropriate privacy and security standards.
