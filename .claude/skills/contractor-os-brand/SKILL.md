# Contractor OS Brand Skill

Contractor OS is contract control for contractor businesses.

Positioning:
Contractor OS helps contractor businesses control live contracts, reduce wasted visits, protect margin, and prove performance by turning messy job information into a clear contract control system.

Tone:
- Practical
- Plain-speaking
- Contractor-led
- Calm
- Professional
- Commercially sharp
- No hype

Do:
- Talk about live jobs, delays, approvals, evidence, repeat visits, client reporting, and margin risk.
- Sell control and clarity.
- Keep human approval around money, scope, safety, client commitments, and risk.
- Treat the Contract Control Review as the first sellable offer.

Do not:
- Lead with AI.
- Sound like a generic SaaS company.
- Invent savings.
- Invent client results.
- Overbuild before customer learning.
- Make the website feel like a tech demo.
