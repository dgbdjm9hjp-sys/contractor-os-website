# Conversion Copy Skill

Write clear buyer-facing copy for practical B2B offers.

Rules:
- Lead with the buyer problem.
- Sell the outcome, not the software.
- Use plain British English.
- Keep sentences short.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid AI-heavy language.
- Avoid unsupported statistics.
- Avoid fake urgency.
- Avoid invented proof.
- Make the CTA clear and repeated.
- Use the buyer's language: delays, approvals, evidence, repeat visits, margin leakage, client reporting, manager chasing.

For Contractor OS:
- Position as contract control.
- Do not position as generic software.
- Do not lead with AI.
- Do not claim fixed savings without baseline data.
- Do not invent case studies.
- The first offer is the Contract Control Review.
